Angel Face

Year Introducted/Discovered: 1968
Color: Pinks. Purples.
Average Dimensions: Height: 3' - 4'(90cm - 120 cm) and 3 ft. wide (90 cm).
Number of Petals: 25 - 30
Spacing: 36"(90 cm)
Cold Hardiness: 5-9
Resistance to Disease: Quite susceptible to a variety of diseases.
Fragrance: Yes. Sweet, citrusy fragrance
Growth: Blooms from Mid Spring to Mid Fall.
Bloom: Budisurn shaped. Medium, double, ruffled blooms.
