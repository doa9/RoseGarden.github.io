Beverly Eleganza

Product Description

Beverly rose is a very fragrant light pink hybrid tea rose growing upright and bushy reaching 4' tall by 3' wide. Beverly displays a fully double bloom with excellent resistance to black spot and mildew.

Year Introducted/Discovered: 2012
Color: Pink
Average Dimensions: 4'x 3'
Number of Petals: Approximately 40
Spacing: 3' - 4'
Cold Hardiness: Plant Hardiness Zone 5-9
Resistance to Disease: Yes
Fragrance: Very
Growth: Upright & bushy
Bloom: Repeat, up to 4
