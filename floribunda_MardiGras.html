Mardi Gras

Year Introducted/Discovered: 2007
Color: Yellow, Orange, Pink
Average Dimensions: 3'-4'
Number of Petals: 20-25
Spacing: 36-48 in.
Cold Hardiness: 4-9
Resistance to Disease: Yes
Fragrance: Light, Spicy
Growth: Tall upright shrub.
Bloom: Generally borne singly or in small clusters.
