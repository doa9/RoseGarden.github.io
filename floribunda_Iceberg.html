Iceberg

Year Introducted/Discovered: 1958
Color: Pure white
Average Dimensions: 3'-5'
Number of Petals: 24-26
Spacing: 3' - 5'
Cold Hardiness: 4-9
Resistance to Disease: Average
Fragrance: Yes
Growth: Vigorous, upright, bushy and tall
Bloom: Long pointed bud. Double blooms in large clusters, recurrent, medium sized, cupped loosely, ruffled.
