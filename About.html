About Dr.Ting Zhang

Dr.Ting Zhang holds a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Alabama. Her main research interests include sensor systems, intelligent algorithms and virtual reality. She has published more than twenty papers on referred journals and conference proceedings, as well as a book. She teaches a wide range of courses in computer science, including computer organization, programming languages, data structures, and web programming. She supervises students on senior projects and research activities.

Topic for Research

Artificial Intelligence Based Web Portal of Flower

In this research, we will build an Artificial Intelligence based web portal for growing flowers. The website is unique that it targets for garden beginners in Texas, and can give recommendations based on user's preference. We will include around 100 species, divided by categories of annuals and perennials. Particularly we will have special categories of roses, cactus and orchids. We will have special categories of climbers. For each plant, we will have information about color category, growing easiness, care tips, blooming periods, budget range, and possible nurseries. Images and videos will be utilized. Moreover, we will have an artificial intelligence based component built on database, to give recommendations, based on user's preference.The proposed research will support Department of Computer Science and Engineering Technology as Computer Science program have just launched a newly approved Master's program, Master's in Artificial Intelligence. The research work will involve around 80 students taking Web Programming class each Fall so that they will apply their knowledge to benefit the community in a very practical way. The research involves novel algorithms in Database Searching, Artificial Intelligence and Web Programming, as booming areas in computer science for people's everyday use. The algorithm and the project can result in patents, in Artificial Intelligence and Web Programming.
